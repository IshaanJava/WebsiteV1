Room 1 & 2 - Sustainable Sweep

In this captivating level, our intrepid pixel art character embarks on a mission of environmental consciousness. Amidst a vibrant, pixelated world, the character navigates through a dungeoness landscape, encountering piles of discarded items. With determination and purpose, they meticulously differentiate between trash and recycling, demonstrating the critical importance of responsible waste management. As the character diligently segregates, a powerful message resonates - every action counts in the pursuit of a greener, more sustainable world. This level serves as an engaging platform to raise awareness about environmental conservatism, inspiring players to adopt eco-friendly habits in both the virtual realm and the real world.
